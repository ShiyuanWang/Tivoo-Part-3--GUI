Title : JD-LLM Lives in International Law: Panel of Alumni
Start Time : 2011/09/30 15:30
End Time : 2011/09/30 16:30
Description : The JD/LLM Program in International and Comparative Law presents a Lives in International Law panel discussion with four JD/LLM alumni: Amber Jordan '10 (United State Court of International Trade); Michael Gilles '10 (U.S. State Department) and Coalter Lathrop '06 (Sovereign Geographic) about a broad array of international opportunities available to graduates pursuing careers in international law. The panelists will speak about their own careers paths and potential opportunities for current students. Light snacks and beverages will be provided. For more information, please contact Oleg Kobelev at kobelev@law.duke.edu.
