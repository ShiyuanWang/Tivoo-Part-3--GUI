Title : An Inner View of Interviews
Start Time : 2011/09/26 18:30

Topics covered will include behavioral interviews, case interviews, cognitive testing, and role play/simulation exercises. Demonstrations will be provided. The presentation will also feature general interview tips designed to give you a competitive edge in your job search. Space is limited!
