Title : Durham Regional and Duke Raleigh Hospitals Fall Run/Walk Club
Start Time : 2011/09/26 17:00
End Time : 2011/09/26 18:00
Description : The Live for Life @ Duke Run/ Walk Club is back and it's not to late to register. Meet your health and fitness goals with the Duke Run/Walk Club whether you are a beginner walker or training for a 5K. Offered each spring and fall, the club is a free, 12-week program for all Duke Faculty and Staff at all fitness levels. The club meets at Durham Regional Employee Entrance and Duke Raleigh Hospital - Wellness Center Entrance every Monday and Wednesday from 5:00 - 6:00 p.m., beginning August 22 through November 9, 2011. This Fall participants can also sign up for the club's "Fitness Fundamentals" workshop. For more information on the Duke Run/Walk Club, please contact LIVE FOR LIFE at (919) 684-3136, option 1 or e-mail Elizabeth Grabosky at elizabeth.grabosky@duke.edu
