Title : Fall Plant Sale
Start Time : 2011/10/01 09:00
End Time : 2011/10/01 12:00
Description : Fall is the ideal time for planting, and Duke Gardens has a wide array of plants for sale, many of them propagated from Duke Gardens' own plants. Join Friends of Duke Gardens to attend the 8 a.m. pre-sale. Duke Gardens, 420 Anderson St.
