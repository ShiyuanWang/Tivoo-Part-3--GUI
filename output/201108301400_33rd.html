Title : 33rd Annual Duke Employee Art Show
Start Time : 2011/08/30 14:00
End Time : 2011/09/29 14:00
Description : In conjunction with the National Arts Program, Health Arts Network at Duke presents the 33rd Annual Duke Employee Art Show! Original works by Duke employees, their immediate family, retirees and volunteers will be on view between August 30 and September 29 in Duke University Medical Center. The Employee Art Show will feature works in Amateur, Intermediate, Professional, Youth and Teen categories. Contact: samantha.cole@duke.edu.
