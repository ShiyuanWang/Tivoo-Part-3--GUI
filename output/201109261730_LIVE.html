Title : LIVE FOR LIFE @ Duke Fall Run/Walk Club
Start Time : 2011/09/26 17:30
End Time : 2011/09/26 18:30
Description : The Live for Life @ Duke Run/ Walk Club is back. Meet your health and fitness goals with the Duke Run/Walk Club whether you are a beginner walker or training for a 5K. Offered each spring and fall, the club is a free, 12-week program for all Duke Faculty and Staff at all fitness levels. The club meets at Wallace Wade Stadium on West Campus; the East Campus wall across from Whole Foods on Broad Street. The Session is every Monday and Wednesday from 5:30 - 6:30 p.m., beginning August 22 through November 9, 2011. This Fall participants can also sign up for the club's "Fitness Fundamentals" workshop. For more information on the Duke Run/Walk Club, please contact LIVE FOR LIFE at (919) 684-3136, option 1 or e-mail Elizabeth Grabosky at elizabeth.grabosky@duke.edu
