Title : RCR Forum-Ethical Issues in Genomics Research and Gene Patents
Start Time : 2011/09/27 15:00
End Time : 2011/09/27 17:00
Description : The field of genome based diagnostics, as we move toward whole-gene sequencing, was impacted by the legal verdict against Myriad Genetics in the case that invalidated patents held by Myriad on the BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes. The legal status of gene patents remains a contentious issue and has implications for researchers and anyone interested in personal genomics.
